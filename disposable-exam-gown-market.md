<p><strong>The Disposable Exam Gown Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paper Based</li><li>Plastic Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable exam gowns can be categorized into two types: paper-based and plastic-based. Paper-based gowns are made from lightweight, breathable materials such as tissue or spunbonded polypropylene. They are more environmentally friendly and often used for low-risk examinations. Plastic-based gowns, on the other hand, are made from materials like polyethylene or polypropylene and offer better protection against fluids and contaminants. They are commonly used in high-risk medical procedures and provide a barrier against infectious agents. The choice between the two types depends on the level of protection required and the specific examination or procedure being performed.</p></p>
