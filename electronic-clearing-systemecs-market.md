<p><p>Electronic Clearing System (ECS) is a digital payment system that allows funds to be transferred electronically between bank accounts. There are two types of ECS markets: ECS Credit and ECS Debit. In the ECS Credit market, funds are transferred from the payer's account to the payee's account. This is typically used for salary payments, dividends, or interest payouts. In the ECS Debit market, funds are transferred from the payee's account to the payer's account, commonly used for bill payments like utility bills or loan repayments. Both ECS Credit and ECS Debit provide a convenient and secure way for individuals and businesses to make and receive payments electronically.</p></p>
