<p><p>The antidote market can be categorized into three main types: chemical antidotes, physical antidotes, and pharmacological antidotes. Chemical antidotes involve the use of specific chemicals that can neutralize the effects of toxins or poisons in the body. Physical antidotes involve physical methods, such as removing the poison from the body or administering supportive treatments. Pharmacological antidotes involve the use of drugs or medications to counteract the effects of a toxic substance. These different market types cater to various situations where antidotes are required for emergency medical interventions.</p></p>
