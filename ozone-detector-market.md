On the other hand, Electrochemistry detectors use an electrochemical cell to measure ozone concentration, providing real-time measurements and versatility in different environments. Both types serve the purpose of detecting ozone levels, but with different approaches and capabilities, catering to different user requirements and preferences.</p></p>
