<p><p>Incremental magnetic rotary encoders are devices that provide information about the position and speed of rotating machinery. They generate output signals based on the movement of a magnetic strip or disk, allowing users to measure and track angular movement. On the other hand, absolute magnetic rotary encoders offer a unique code for each position along the rotation, providing absolute position information without the need for a reference point. These encoders are widely used in various applications such as motor control, robotics, industrial automation, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
