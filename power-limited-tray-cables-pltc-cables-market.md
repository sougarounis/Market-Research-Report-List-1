<p><p>Power Limited Tray Cables (PLTC Cables) are specifically designed for use in industrial applications where protection against electrical interference is necessary. The market for PLTC cables includes three types: Overall Shielded Pairs/Triads, Individual Shielded Pairs, and Overall Shielded Pairs. Overall Shielded Pairs/Triads cables provide shielding for the entire cable, protecting against external interference. Individual Shielded Pairs cables have shielding around each pair of wires, providing individual protection. Overall Shielded Pairs cables offer shielding for the entire cable, but not for individual pairs within it. These different types cater to specific needs in terms of electrical interference protection.</p></p>
