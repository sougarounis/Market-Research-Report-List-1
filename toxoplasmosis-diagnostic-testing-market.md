<p><ul><li>Indirect Immune Fluorescent Test (IFAT)</li><li>Modified Agglutination Test (MAT)</li><li>Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Immunoglobulin Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Toxoplasmosis diagnostic testing market comprises various types of tests for detecting the infection caused by Toxoplasma gondii parasite. Indirect Immune Fluorescent Test (IFAT) uses fluorescent dye markers to detect specific antibodies in the blood. The Modified Agglutination Test (MAT) detects antibodies by causing clumping of particles in the blood sample. The Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) detects specific antibodies using enzyme reactions. Immunoglobulin tests measure the levels of immunoglobulins in the blood, which can indicate past or recent infection. These tests aid in timely diagnosis and treatment of the disease</p></p>
