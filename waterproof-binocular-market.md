<p><p>The waterproof binocular market offers various types of binoculars based on prism designs. Roof prism binoculars have a streamlined design and offer a straight-through optical path for compactness. Porro prism binoculars have a classic design with an offset optical path for a wider field of view. Abbe-Koenig prism binoculars use a complex optical system for high performance in a compact form. Other prism designs may include Schmidt-Pechan, field flattener, or Galilean prisms. These different prism types offer consumers a range of options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
