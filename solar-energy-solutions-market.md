<p><p>The solar energy solutions market is comprised of various types of modules. Mono-Si modules, made with single crystalline silicon, offer high efficiency and reliability. Multi-Si modules use multiple crystalline silicon wafers and are cost-effective. CdTe modules utilize cadmium telluride semiconductor technology, offering good performance in low-light conditions. CIGS modules use copper indium gallium selenide thin film technology, providing flexibility and lightweight designs. a-Si modules, made with amorphous silicon, are low in cost but less efficient. Additionally, there are other modules available, such as organic solar cells and perovskite modules, which have their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
