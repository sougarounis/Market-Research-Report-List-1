<p><p>Hydraulic flange spreaders are devices used to separate or spread flanges in various industrial applications. There are two types of hydraulic flange spreaders: split type and integral type. </p><p>1. Split type flange spreaders consist of two separate halves that can be bolted together or attached to a flange, expanding to separate the flange joint. This type offers flexibility in terms of usage as they can fit different sizes of flanges.</p><p>2. Integral type flange spreaders have a fixed body and are typically used for one specific flange size. They are more compact and easier to use due to their single-piece design.</p></p>
