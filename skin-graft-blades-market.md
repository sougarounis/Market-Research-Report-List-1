<p><p>The skin graft blades market encompasses two main types: full-thickness and split-thickness skin grafts. Full-thickness skin grafts involve transferring the full thickness of the skin from a donor site to a recipient site, typically used for smaller wounds or cosmetic reconstructions. On the other hand, split-thickness skin grafts involve removing only a portion of the skin, including the epidermis and a portion of the dermis, and are commonly used for larger wounds or burns. These blades play a crucial role in achieving successful skin graft procedures, aiding in precise and effective grafting procedures.</p></p>
