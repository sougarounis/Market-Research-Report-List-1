<p><p>Portable Image Printers Market Types - Sublimation Printer, Inkjet Printer</p><p>The portable image printers market offers two primary types of printers - sublimation printers and inkjet printers. Sublimation printers utilize a process that involves converting solid ink into a gas, which is then transferred onto the printing medium, resulting in high-quality and durable prints. On the other hand, inkjet printers use liquid ink that is sprayed onto the paper, providing a cost-effective solution for printing images and documents. Both types of printers offer convenience and portability, allowing users to easily print images on the go.</p></p>
