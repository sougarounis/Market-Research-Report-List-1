<p><p>The upright commercial vacuums market can be classified into two types: single-motor and dual-motor. Single-motor vacuums have a single motor that drives both the brush and the vacuum suction. They are generally lightweight and more affordable than dual-motor vacuums. On the other hand, dual-motor vacuums have separate motors for the brush and vacuum, which allows for more powerful suction and efficient cleaning. These vacuums are typically heavier and may be more expensive compared to single-motor vacuums.</p></p>
