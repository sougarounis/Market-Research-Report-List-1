<p><p>Composite LPG cylinders are available in two major market types: Metal Tank Winding and Composite Material Tank Winding. Metal Tank Winding refers to LPG cylinders constructed with a metal liner, such as steel or aluminum, over which composite materials are wound and then bonded. On the other hand, Composite Material Tank Winding refers to cylinders that are solely constructed using composite materials like fiberglass reinforced plastic. These market types offer different options to cater to various needs and preferences in the Composite LPG Cylinders industry.</p></p>
