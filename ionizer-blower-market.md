<p><p>Ionizer blowers are devices used to remove static electricity from objects and surfaces. The stand blower type is a standalone unit that can be placed on a table or floor. It blows ionized air onto the targeted area, neutralizing any static charges. On the other hand, the overhead blower is mounted on the ceiling or wall and directs ionized air downwards. It is commonly used in manufacturing environments where objects on conveyor belts or workstations need continuous static elimination. Both types of ionizer blowers effectively eliminate static electricity, but differ in their placement and direction of airflow.</p></p>
