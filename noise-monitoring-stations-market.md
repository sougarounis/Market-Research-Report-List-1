<p><p>The market for Noise Monitoring Stations can be divided into two main types: Portable Noise Monitoring Systems and Permanent Noise Monitoring Systems. Portable systems are designed to be easily moved and deployed in different locations to capture noise data for short periods of time. They are commonly used for temporary measurements or when monitoring specific events. On the other hand, Permanent Noise Monitoring Systems are installed in fixed locations and continuously monitor noise levels over an extended period. These systems are commonly used for long-term environmental or community noise monitoring.</p></p>
