<p><p>The swimming costume dryer market consists of two main types: rotation type and drum type. Rotation type dryers use a rotating mechanism that spins the swimsuit to dry it faster. These dryers are compact and ideal for individual use. On the other hand, drum type dryers are larger and have a drum-like structure where the swimming costume is placed for drying. They are more suitable for commercial use in places like swimming pools or laundromats, as they can handle multiple garments simultaneously. Both types offer efficient drying solutions for swimming costumes, catering to different user needs.</p></p>
