<p><p>Center punches are tools used in metalworking to create a guide mark or indentation on a workpiece. There are two main market types for center punches: pneumatic and automatic. Pneumatic center punches are operated by compressed air and offer high power and precision. They are commonly used in industrial settings where large volumes of workpieces need to be marked quickly. On the other hand, automatic center punches utilize spring-loaded mechanisms to deliver a forceful impact. Ideal for smaller-scale applications, automatic center punches are handheld and allow for easy one-handed operation.</p></p>
