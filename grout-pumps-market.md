<p><p>Grout pumps are used in construction for injecting or pumping grout into structures. There are two main types of grout pump models: single and double. Single grout pump models feature a single pump that is capable of injecting a specific amount of grout at a time. Double grout pump models, on the other hand, have two pumps that can simultaneously inject separate amounts of grout. This allows for increased efficiency and greater productivity compared to single grout pump models.</p></p>
