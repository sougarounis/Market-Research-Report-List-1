<p><p>The Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) Filters Market Types include Initial Filter and Fine Filter. The Initial Filter is designed to remove large particles of dirt, rust, and scale from the CNG stream. It protects downstream components from damage and blockage. On the other hand, the Fine Filter is used to remove smaller contaminants like oil and moisture, ensuring the overall purity of the CNG. These filters play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of CNG-powered vehicles and equipment by ensuring the clean and reliable operation of the system.</p></p>
