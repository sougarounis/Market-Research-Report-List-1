<p><p>The remote monitoring equipment market can be classified into two types: external and implantable. External remote monitoring equipment refers to devices that are placed on or near the body's surface to monitor various physiological parameters. These devices are non-invasive and often used for short-term monitoring. On the other hand, implantable remote monitoring equipment is surgically inserted into the body to continuously monitor specific functions or conditions. These devices provide long-term monitoring capabilities and are often used for chronic conditions or post-surgical care.</p></p>
