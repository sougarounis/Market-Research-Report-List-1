<p><p>Cable conduit systems are used to protect and manage electrical cables in various applications. The market includes two main types: rigid cable conduit system and flexible cable conduit system. Rigid cable conduit systems are made of rigid materials like metal or PVC, offering high durability and protection. They are often used in commercial and industrial settings. On the other hand, flexible cable conduit systems are made of flexible materials, such as plastic or metal with a spiraling design.
