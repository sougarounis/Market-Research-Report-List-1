Polymer Printers, on the other hand, are used to manufacture objects using polymers or plastic materials through processes like fused deposition modeling or stereolithography. Lastly, the Other market includes printers that utilize unconventional materials such as ceramics or composites, or printers that employ different technologies not covered by metal or polymer printers.</p></p>
