There are three main types of digital refractometers in the market: handheld, benchtop, and inline process refractometers. Handheld refractometers are portable and commonly used in field applications. Benchtop refractometers are larger and typically used in laboratories for more precise measurements. Inline process refractometers are designed for continuous monitoring and control of liquid processes in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical.</p></p>
