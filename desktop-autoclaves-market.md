<p><p>Desktop autoclaves are sterilization devices used to eliminate microorganisms and pathogens from medical instruments and equipment. There are various types of desktop autoclaves, including steam autoclaves, plasma autoclaves, hot air autoclaves, and others. Steam autoclaves use high-pressure steam to generate heat and moisture for sterilization. Plasma autoclaves use low-temperature plasma gas to kill microorganisms. Hot air autoclaves utilize dry heat for sterilization. Other types may include ethylene oxide autoclaves or chemical vapor autoclaves, which use specific chemicals for sterilization. Each type offers different advantages and cater to specific sterilization requirements.</p></p>
