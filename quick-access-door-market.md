Two common types in the market are the Viewpoint Type and Blank Flange Type.</p><p>Viewpoint Type: This type of quick access door is equipped with a transparent viewport, allowing users to visually inspect equipment or processes without the need to open the door fully. It provides convenience and safety by eliminating the need for frequent door opening and minimizing potential hazards.</p><p>Blank Flange Type: The blank flange type of quick access door is a solid panel without any window or visible opening. It is often used in applications where visual inspection is not required, but quick and secure access is necessary for maintenance, repairs, or emergency situations. This type ensures maximum protection and minimizes the risk of accidents or equipment damage.</p></p>
