<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive MOSFETs market includes three major types: N-Channel, P-Channel, and Dual N and P-Channel. N-Channel MOSFETs are the most common type, commonly used for switching applications due to their low on-resistance and high efficiency. P-Channel MOSFETs, on the other hand, are used for high-side switching applications and are known for their lower on-resistance compared to N-Channel MOSFETs. Dual N and P-Channel MOSFETs combine both types in a single package, providing flexibility for both high-side and low-side switching applications.</p></p>
