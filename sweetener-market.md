<p><p>Sweeteners are food additives used to enhance the taste of various food and beverages while providing lesser calories compared to traditional sugar. Artificial sweeteners, such as aspartame, saccharin, and sucralose, are chemically synthesized and are non-nutritive.</p><p>The Sweetener Market has been experiencing remarkable growth in recent years. The rising prevalence of obesity and diabetes, along with the growing health-consciousness among consumers, has led to an increased demand for low-calorie and sugar-free food and beverages. Stevia, in particular, has gained significant popularity as a natural sweetener due to its low-calorie content and its ability to maintain stable blood sugar levels.</p><p>Furthermore, the Sweetener Market has witnessed various trends that have influenced its growth. The rising adoption of sugar substitutes in the pharmaceutical industry for the manufacturing of medicines and syrups has created lucrative opportunities for the market.
