Made from bioabsorbable materials like polylactic acid (PLA) or poly-l-lactide (PLLA), these screws provide initial anchorage and maintain graft fixation while gradually degrading and being absorbed by the body over time.</p><p>The absorbable interference screw market has witnessed substantial growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period.
