<p><p>Myopia lens, also known as nearsightedness lens, is a corrective lens used to correct myopia or nearsightedness. Myopia is a common refractive error where distant objects appear blurry, while close-up objects are clear. Myopia lens helps to focus light on the retina properly, allowing individuals with myopia to see distant objects more clearly.</p><p>In recent years, there has been a significant increase in the prevalence of myopia globally, especially among children and young adults. Factors such as increased screen time, reduced outdoor activities, and genetics contribute to the rise in myopia cases. This increasing myopia prevalence is driving the demand for myopia lens.</p><p>The future outlook of the myopia lens market is highly positive. The market is expected to witness substantial growth due to the growing number of myopia cases, advancements in lens technology, and increasing awareness about the benefits of myopia correction.
