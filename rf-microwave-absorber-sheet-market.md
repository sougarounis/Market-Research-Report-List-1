<p><p>Low permeability RF microwave absorber sheets are specifically designed to minimize the amount of electromagnetic radiation that can pass through them. These sheets are made from materials that have low conductivity and dielectric constant, allowing them to effectively absorb and dissipate electromagnetic energy. On the other hand, high permeability RF microwave absorber sheets are made from materials that have high magnetic permeability, enabling them to effectively absorb and redirect electromagnetic energy. These sheets are suitable for applications where a higher level of absorption and redirection of electromagnetic waves is required.</p></p>
