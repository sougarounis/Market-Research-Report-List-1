<p><p>Anti-static spray is a substance used to eliminate or reduce static electricity on various surfaces. The spray typically contains conductive agents that neutralize the static charges and ensure smooth flow of electricity.</p><p>The current outlook for the anti-static spray market is positive and is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years. With advancements in technology and increasing awareness of static-related issues, the demand for anti-static solutions is expected to rise.
