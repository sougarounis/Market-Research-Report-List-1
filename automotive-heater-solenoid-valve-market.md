<p><p>Automotive Heater Solenoid Valve market consists of two main types: Direct Acting Type and Internally Piloted Type. Direct Acting Type valves operate by the movement of a plunger directly controlling the flow of fluid. In contrast, Internally Piloted Type valves use a small pilot valve to control the flow of fluid within the main valve. Both types are essential components in automotive heating systems, allowing for precise control of coolant flow, and maintaining optimal temperature within the vehicle.</p></p>
