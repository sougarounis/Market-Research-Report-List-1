<p><p>The synthetic surgical glue market includes different types of glues such as cyanoacrylate, polymeric hydrogel, and urethane-based adhesive. Urethane-based adhesive is a durable glue that is resistant to various conditions and provides excellent adhesion. These glues are used in surgical procedures to seal wounds and incisions, providing a secure and efficient alternative to traditional sutures.</p></p>
