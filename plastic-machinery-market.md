<p><p>The plastic machinery market includes various types of machines used in the manufacturing of plastic products. Injection molding machines are used to produce plastic parts through the injection of molten plastic into a mold. Extruder machines are used to shape plastic materials into continuous profiles or tubes. Blow molding machines are employed to create hollow plastic products by inflating a hot plastic tube into a mold. Rolling mill machines flatten metal into sheets or strips. The "others" category includes different types of plastic machinery not specifically mentioned before.</p></p>
