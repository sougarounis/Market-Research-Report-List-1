<p><p>Allied healthcare staffing market consists of two main types: temporary and permanent. Temporary staffing refers to the provision of healthcare professionals on a short-term basis, usually to cover a specific period of high demand or to fill in for staff absences. These workers are often employed by staffing agencies and may work on a contract or per-diem basis. Permanent staffing, on the other hand, involves the recruitment and hiring of healthcare professionals as full-time or part-time employees directly by healthcare organizations. These workers become part of the organization's regular staff and have longer-term job security.</p></p>
