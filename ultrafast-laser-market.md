<p><p>The Ultrafast Laser market consists of two types: Femtosecond Laser and Picosecond Laser. Femtosecond Laser refers to lasers that emit ultrafast pulses lasting one quadrillionth of a second, enabling precise and intricate surgeries, material processing, and scientific research. On the other hand, Picosecond Laser emits ultrafast pulses lasting one trillionth of a second, offering similar applications but with slightly less precision. These lasers find applications in industries such as healthcare, electronics, and manufacturing, where high-speed and accurate processes are required.</p></p>
