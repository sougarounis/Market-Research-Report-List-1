Nd:YVO4 is a widely used crystal due to its high absorption and emission cross-sections, making it suitable for high-power laser applications. Nd:GdVO4 offers similar properties to Nd:YVO4 but with enhanced thermal conductivity, which enables better heat dissipation. Nd:LuVO4 is known for its high ionic conductivity, making it ideal for low-threshold laser applications. These crystals play a crucial role in various industries, including telecommunications, medical technology, and scientific research.</p></p>
