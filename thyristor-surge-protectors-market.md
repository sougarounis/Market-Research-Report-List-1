<p><p>Thyristor surge protectors, also known as thyristor surge suppressors or thyristor surge arrestors, are electrical devices designed to protect electronic equipment from high voltage transients, surges, and spikes. They are commonly used in power supply systems, telecommunication networks, industrial facilities, and other applications where electrical equipment is exposed to transient voltage events that can cause damage.</p><p>These surge protectors work by diverting excess voltage and current away from sensitive equipment, thus preventing it from being damaged. They do this by using thyristors, which are semiconductor devices that can rapidly switch on and off in response to voltage changes. When a transient voltage surge occurs, the thyristor surge protector triggers and provides a low-impedance path for the excess voltage, effectively clamping it to a safe level.</p><p>The thyristor surge protectors market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for high-quality power supply systems and the rising adoption of electronic devices in various industries. The growing use of sensitive electronic equipment and the need to protect them from voltage transients and spikes are driving the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing emphasis on reducing downtime and minimizing equipment damage caused by voltage disturbances is further fueling the demand for thyristor surge protectors.
