<p><strong>The In-vitro Toxicity Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Well Plate System</li><li>Multi-compartmental Perfused Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-vitro toxicity testing market includes various types of technologies used to assess the potential toxicity of substances. One type is the static well plate system, which involves culturing cells or tissues in a single well of a plate and assessing the effects of a substance on them. Another type is the multi-compartmental perfused system, where cells or tissues are cultured in separate compartments connected by a flow of media, allowing for more complex assessments of substance toxicity. Both these systems are used in research and pharmaceutical industries for testing the safety of new drug candidates.</p></p>
