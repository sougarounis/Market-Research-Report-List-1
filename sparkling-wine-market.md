<p><strong>The Sparkling Wine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal sparkling wine</li><li>Semi-sparkling wine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sparkling wine refers to a type of wine that contains carbon dioxide, creating bubbles and effervescence. Normal sparkling wine is fully carbonated, resulting in a high level of bubbles and a fizzy texture. It is typically produced using the traditional method, where secondary fermentation occurs in the bottle. Semi-sparkling wine, on the other hand, contains a lower level of carbonation and fewer bubbles. It is usually produced using the Charmat method, where secondary fermentation occurs in large tanks. Semi-sparkling wine is often considered to have a lighter and less intense effervescence compared to normal sparkling wine.</p></p>
