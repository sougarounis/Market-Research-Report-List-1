LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays use tiny LED bulbs to create images and are known for their high brightness and energy efficiency. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays employ organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, resulting in better contrast and thinness. These display technologies play a crucial role in the visual experience of electronic devices such as TVs, smartphones, and computer monitors.</p></p>
