<p><p>The traditional wiper system market refers to the conventional windshield wiper systems that have been used in vehicles for many years. These systems operate manually and require the driver to turn them on/off as needed.</p><p>On the other hand, the rain sensing wiper system market involves advanced technology that automatically detects the presence of rain or moisture on the windshield. These systems feature sensors that activate the wipers when rain is detected and adjust their speed according to the intensity of the rainfall.</p><p>Both types of wiper systems serve the purpose of ensuring clear visibility through the windshield, with the rain sensing wiper system providing additional convenience by eliminating the need for manual operation.</p></p>
