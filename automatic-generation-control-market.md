<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Generation Control Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbine-Governor Control (TGC)</li><li>Load-Frequency Control (LFC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Generation Control (AGC) market consists of two main types: Turbine-Governor Control (TGC) and Load-Frequency Control (LFC). TGC involves regulating the power output of individual turbines or generators to maintain system frequency and stability. It ensures that power generation matches the load demand. On the other hand, LFC focuses on monitoring the total power output of all generators and adjusting it to meet the constantly changing load demand. Both TGC and LFC play crucial roles in maintaining grid stability and the reliability of power systems.</p></p>
