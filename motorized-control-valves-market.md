<p><p>Motorized control valves control the flow of fluids or gases in industrial processes. There are various types, including butterfly valves, ball valves, globe valves, and others. Butterfly valves have a rotating disc to regulate flow, ball valves use a perforated ball to control flow, and globe valves have a movable disk that aligns with the flow. Other types include diaphragm valves and plug valves. These valves are commonly used in industries like oil and gas, water and wastewater, chemical, and power generation for precise and automated control of fluid or gas flow.</p></p>
