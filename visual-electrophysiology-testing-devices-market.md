<p><p>The Visual Electrophysiology Testing Devices market comprises various types of devices used for diagnosing eye disorders. Multifocal Electroretinogram devices measure electrical responses in the retina, providing information about localized retinal function. Visual-Evoked Response devices evaluate the electrical signals generated in the visual pathway, aiding in the detection of optic nerve diseases. Electroretinogram devices assess the electrical activity of the retina, helping diagnose retinal disorders. Electro-Oculogram devices measure eye movement and evaluate eye muscle function, aiding in the diagnosis of eye movement disorders.</p></p>
