<p><p>Thumb suspenders refer to a type of support product for the thumb joint. Splint type thumb suspenders are designed to immobilize the thumb, providing stability and preventing further injury. These are commonly used in cases of fractures or sprains. On the other hand, support strap type thumb suspenders offer a flexible and adjustable compression to the thumb, aiding in pain relief and enhancing joint function. They are often used for conditions like arthritis or tendonitis. Both types of thumb suspenders cater to different needs and provide essential support for thumb-related issues.</p></p>
