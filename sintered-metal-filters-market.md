Medium porosity filters have a porosity between 30-60%, which provides a moderate level of pore connections. High porosity filters have a porosity above 60%, indicating the highest number of interconnected pores. These filters vary in their pore structure, influencing their filtration efficiency and the size of particles they can effectively remove.</p></p>
