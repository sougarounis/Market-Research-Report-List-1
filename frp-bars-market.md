<p><strong>The FRP Bars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GFRP Bars</li><li>CFRP Bars</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FRP bars market comprises different types of fiber reinforced polymer (FRP) materials used in construction. GFRP bars, or glass fiber reinforced polymer bars, are made from glass fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. They offer corrosion resistance and high strength. CFRP bars, or carbon fiber reinforced polymer bars, are made from carbon fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. They provide excellent tensile strength and low thermal expansion. The "Others" market category includes FRP bars made from other fibers, such as basalt or aramid fibers. These FRP bars offer specific properties suited for various applications.</p></p>
