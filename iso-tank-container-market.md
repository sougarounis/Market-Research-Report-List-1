<p><p>An ISO tank container, also known as an ISO tank, is a type of shipping container manufactured according to the standards set by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO). These containers are equipped with various safety features, including pressure relief devices, insulation, and heating systems, to ensure the integrity and security of the cargo being transported.</p><p>The ISO tank container market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. Several factors contribute to this market growth. Firstly, the rising demand for bulk transportation of chemicals, gases, and other hazardous materials is driving the adoption of ISO tank containers. Additionally, the increase in international trade and global economic growth has led to an upsurge in cross-border transportation, further bolstering the market demand.</p><p>Furthermore, the advantages offered by ISO tank containers, such as reduced transportation costs, enhanced safety, and higher operational efficiency, are driving their preference over other modes of transportation. The ability to easily switch between different modes of transport without the need for cargo transfer also adds to their popularity.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing focus on the development of lightweight and eco-friendly ISO tank containers. Additionally, innovations such as smart sensors and tracking systems are being incorporated into ISO tank containers to enable real-time monitoring and optimize logistics operations.</p><p>In conclusion, the ISO tank container market is experiencing steady growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period.
