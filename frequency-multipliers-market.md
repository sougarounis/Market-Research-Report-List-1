<p><strong>The Frequency Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Frequency Multipliers</li><li>Passive Frequency Multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency multipliers are devices used in electronics to generate higher frequencies from lower input frequencies. Active frequency multipliers consist of active components, such as transistors or amplifiers, which amplify and multiply the input signal. These are commonly used in applications like wireless communications. Passive frequency multipliers, on the other hand, rely on passive components like diodes or capacitors to perform multiplication without amplification. They are often used in microwave applications. Both types serve distinct purposes and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
