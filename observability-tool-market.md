<p><strong>The Observability Tool Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metrics</li><li>Logs</li><li>Traces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The observability tool market consists of three key types: metrics, logs, and traces. Metrics provide quantitative data on system performance, offering insights into resource usage, response times, and more. Logs capture detailed records of system events, enabling troubleshooting and issue analysis. Traces focus on individual requests and transactions, allowing for end-to-end visibility and identification of bottlenecks or errors. These three market segments cater to different aspects of observability, empowering organizations to proactively monitor, analyze, and optimize their systems for enhanced performance and reliability.</p></p>
