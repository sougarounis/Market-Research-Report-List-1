<p><p>Unified Communications (UC) software is available in two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based UC software is hosted and maintained by a third-party provider, allowing users to access it remotely over the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and cost savings as the provider handles infrastructure and updates. On the other hand, on-premise UC software is hosted and managed within an organization's own servers or data centers. It provides more control, customization, and security, but requires higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance by the organization.</p></p>
