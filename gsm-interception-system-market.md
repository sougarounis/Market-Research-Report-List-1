<p><strong>The GSM Interception System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Interception System</li><li>Passive Interception System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GSM Interception System market includes two main types: Active Interception System and Passive Interception System. Active Interception Systems are designed to actively intercept and monitor GSM communication by accessing the network directly. They can intercept calls, text messages, and other data in real-time. On the other hand, Passive Interception Systems are designed to passively capture and record GSM communication without directly accessing the network. They are more discreet and can be used for post-event analysis or evidence gathering. Both types serve different purposes in the field of GSM interception.</p></p>
