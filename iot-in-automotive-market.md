<p><p>In the automotive market, the Internet of Things (IoT) can be categorized into three types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded IoT refers to devices with built-in connectivity features, allowing communication and data exchange within vehicles. Tethered IoT involves connecting external devices to a vehicle's system, such as smartphones for accessing certain functions. Integrated IoT combines both embedded and tethered approaches, enabling seamless connectivity and interoperability between various vehicle components and external devices. These three types of IoT solutions play a significant role in enhancing functionality, safety, efficiency, and user experience in the automotive industry.</p></p>
