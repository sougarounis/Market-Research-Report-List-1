<p><p>The multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLCC) market can be divided into two main types: Class 1 and Class 2. Class 1 MLCCs are typically used in applications that require high stability, low losses, and low noise. They offer a high capacitance value but have lower voltage ratings. On the other hand, Class 2 MLCCs are mainly used in applications that demand higher capacitance values and have higher voltage ratings. Although Class 2 MLCCs have slightly higher losses and noise, they are cost-effective and widely used in various electronic devices.</p></p>
