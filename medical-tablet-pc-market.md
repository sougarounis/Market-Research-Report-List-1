<p><p>The medical tablet PC market can be classified into three types based on screen size: below 10 inches, 10-15 inches, and above 15 inches. Tablets below 10 inches are smaller and more portable, suitable for point-of-care tasks like gathering patient information directly bedside. Tablets with screen sizes between 10-15 inches offer a balance between portability and viewing experience, making them versatile for clinical documentation, image viewing, and video consultations. Tablets above 15 inches provide a larger display area, ideal for detailed medical imaging, surgical planning, and educational purposes in medical institutions.</p></p>
