<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis Equipment</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dialysis equipment market consists of two main types: hemodialysis equipment and peritoneal dialysis equipment. Hemodialysis equipment is used for removing waste fluids and toxins from the blood by filtering it outside the body. It involves the use of a dialyzer machine and a vascular access point. On the other hand, peritoneal dialysis equipment allows dialysis to be carried out within the body. It utilizes a catheter to infuse and drain dialysis fluid into the abdominal cavity, utilizing the peritoneum's natural filtering ability.</p></p>
