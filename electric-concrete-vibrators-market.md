<p><strong>The Electric Concrete Vibrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Vibrator</li><li>External Vibrator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Concrete Vibrators market consists of various types of vibrators used for concrete compaction. Internal vibrators, also known as immersion or poker vibrators, are inserted into the concrete to eliminate air bubbles and ensure uniformity. External vibrators, on the other hand, are placed on the outside of the formwork to vibrate the entire structure. Other types of vibrators include surface vibrators, which are used for thin concrete layers, and formwork vibrators for removing air bubbles from the formwork. These different types cater to various construction needs for efficient concrete compaction.</p></p>
