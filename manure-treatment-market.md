<p><p>The manure treatment market can be segmented into two types: biological means and physical means. Physical means, on the other hand, employ various techniques like solid-liquid separation, composting, and drying to remove solid impurities and reduce the volume of manure, making it easier to handle and transport. Both these methods contribute to improving manure management, reducing environmental pollution, and producing valuable resources from waste.</p></p>
