<p><p>Cast iron cylinder sleeves are used in engine cylinders to provide durability and improve performance. There are two types of market offerings in the cast iron cylinder sleeves market: dry liners and wet liners. Dry liners are designed to be pressed into the cylinder block and do not come into direct contact with the coolant. Wet liners, on the other hand, have contact with the coolant and are designed to be installed by placing them directly in the cylinder block bore. Both types offer their own unique advantages and suitability for different engine applications.</p></p>
