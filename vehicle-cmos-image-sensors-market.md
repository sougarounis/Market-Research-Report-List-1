<p><p>Vehicle CMOS image sensors are advanced imaging devices that are specifically designed and used in automotive applications. The increasing demand for advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and the rising trend of integrating cameras in vehicles for safety purposes are the major factors driving the growth of this market. These advancements enable better image quality and enhanced performance under challenging lighting conditions, thereby increasing the adoption of vehicle CMOS image sensors.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising popularity of autonomous vehicles and the increasing investment in electric vehicles are expected to create lucrative opportunities for the market growth. Moreover, the demand for smaller and compact image sensors is also on the rise, as it allows for easy integration into vehicle designs without compromising on performance.</p><p>In conclusion, the vehicle CMOS image sensors market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period, driven by the increasing adoption of ADAS and the growing focus on vehicle safety.
