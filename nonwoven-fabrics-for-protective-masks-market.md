<p><p>Nonwoven fabrics are widely used in production of protective masks. Spunbond nonwoven fabric is durable and has good breathability, making it suitable for the outer layer of masks. Meltblown nonwoven fabric, on the other hand, provides excellent filtration efficiency due to its fine fibers. It is commonly used as the middle layer in masks to trap tiny particles. The market for both types of nonwoven fabrics is significant, with spunbond nonwoven finding application in various industries and meltblown nonwoven witnessing high demand for mask production due to its filtration properties.</p></p>
