<p><p>Frame scaffolding, also known as modular scaffolding or H-frame scaffolding, is a temporary structure commonly used in construction projects to provide support and access to workers and materials. It consists of a set of interconnected metal frames that create a stable and secure platform for workers and tools. Frame scaffolding is widely used due to its versatility, ease of assembly, and durability.</p><p>The global frame scaffolding market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The key factors driving market growth include expanding infrastructure development, increasing construction activities in emerging economies, and rising safety regulations in the construction industry. Additionally, the growing demand for residential and commercial spaces, along with the need for frequent maintenance and repair work, is boosting the adoption of frame scaffolding.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a growing demand for lightweight and portable frame scaffolding systems, as they offer easier transportation and assembly. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing advanced frame scaffolding materials, such as aluminum and fiberglass, which provide higher strength and durability.
