<p><p>Supermarket turnstiles are various types of barriers used to control the flow of people entering and exiting supermarkets. Arm turnstiles feature rotating arms that allow one person to pass through at a time. Swing gates are hinged barriers that can open in both directions, enabling smoother movement. Waist-high helicopter turnstiles have a rotating top section resembling helicopter blades. Other types include optical turnstiles, tripod turnstiles, and full-height turnstiles. These turnstiles help improve security and regulate customer traffic in supermarkets, maintaining order and preventing unauthorized access.</p></p>
