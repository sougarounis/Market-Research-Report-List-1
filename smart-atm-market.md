<p><p>A Smart ATM, also known as an Intelligent ATM, is an advanced version of traditional ATM machines that provides additional functionalities to enhance user experience and security. Biometric technologies such as fingerprint and iris recognition are being incorporated into Smart ATMs to provide secure identification and prevent unauthorized access. This feature enhances user security and eliminates the need for PIN codes or cards.</p><p>Another trend is the adoption of video conferencing capabilities in Smart ATMs. This enables users to connect with customer support representatives remotely, providing them with real-time assistance and reducing the need for physical bank visits.</p><p>Moreover, Smart ATMs are being equipped with advanced cash deposit and withdrawal options. These machines offer the ability to accept cash and checks for deposit, dispense cash in different denominations, and even allow users to select the preferred denominations.</p><p>In summary, the Smart ATM market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for enhanced banking services, convenience, and security.
