<p><p>The Patient Lifting Devices market includes two main types: Manual Lifting Devices and Power Lifting Devices. These devices usually involve the use of handles, levers, or straps to help distribute the patient's weight effectively. On the other hand, Power Lifting Devices utilize electric or hydraulic systems to lift and transfer patients with minimal physical effort from the healthcare staff. These devices are typically powered by battery or electricity and offer increased convenience and efficiency in patient handling.</p></p>
