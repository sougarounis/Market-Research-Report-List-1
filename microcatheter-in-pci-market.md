<p><p>The dual lumen microcatheter and single lumen microcatheter are types of microcatheters used in Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) procedures. The dual lumen microcatheter consists of two lumens, allowing for simultaneous injection and aspiration of fluids or substances. On the other hand, the single lumen microcatheter has a single channel for the injection or aspiration of fluids. Both types of microcatheters are utilized in PCI procedures to access and treat blocked or narrowed coronary arteries.</p></p>
