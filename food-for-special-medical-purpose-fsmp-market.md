<p><p>Food for Special Medical Purpose (FSMP) is a category of specially formulated dietary products intended for individuals with specific medical conditions. The types of FSMP include Complete Nutrition, which provides all essential nutrients for individuals with malabsorption or digestive disorders. Disease Specific Nutrition targets specific medical conditions, such as renal or hepatic impairment, by modifying nutrient composition. Incomplete Nutrition refers to FSMP products that provide only a subset of essential nutrients for individuals with certain dietary restrictions or allergies. These categories of FSMP cater to the diverse nutritional needs of individuals with various medical conditions.</p></p>
