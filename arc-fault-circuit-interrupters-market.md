<p><p>Arc Fault Circuit Interrupters (AFCIs) are electrical devices designed to detect and prevent electrical fires caused by arc faults in residential and commercial buildings. Arc faults occur when there is an unintentional discharge of electrical current between conductors, which can result in overheating, sparks, and fires. AFCIs are installed in circuit breaker panels and monitor the flow of current, automatically shutting off the power to the circuit if an arc fault is detected.</p><p>The Arc Fault Circuit Interrupters Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
