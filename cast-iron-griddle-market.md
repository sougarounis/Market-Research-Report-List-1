Additionally, the growing awareness of health-conscious cooking methods, where excess oils and fats can be eliminated, has further boosted the market.</p><p>Moreover, cast iron griddles are becoming increasingly popular among professional chefs and restaurateurs due to their ability to handle high heat and effectively sear meat while preserving moisture.
