<p><p>A hollow fiber tangential flow cartridge is a filtration device commonly used in biopharmaceutical production processes. The cartridge consists of a bundle of hollow fibers, which allow the liquid to flow tangentially across the membrane surface while retaining the impurities.</p><p>The market for hollow fiber tangential flow cartridges is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
