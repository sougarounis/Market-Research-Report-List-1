Additionally, the shift towards catheter-based ablation systems has also facilitated the growth of the market, as these systems offer greater access to hard-to-reach areas and allow for more targeted therapy.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing demand for minimally invasive procedures, which is expected to drive the adoption of ablation equipment.
