<p><strong>The 3D Visual Perception Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>iToF</li><li>dToF</li><li>Lidar</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D visual perception products market consists of various types, including structured light, iToF (indirect Time-of-Flight), dToF (direct Time-of-Flight), Lidar, and others. Structured light technology utilizes patterns of light and sensors to measure depth and create 3D models. iToF employs indirect measurement of reflected light to determine distances and construct 3D imagery. dToF, on the other hand, measures the time needed for light to travel to an object and back, allowing for accurate 3D representation. Lidar employs lasers to measure distances, enabling detailed depth perception. Lastly, the 'others' category encompasses additional technologies used for 3D visual perception.</p></p>
