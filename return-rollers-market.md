<p><p>Return rollers are an important component of conveyor systems, designed to support and guide the return side of the belt. Disc return rollers consist of a series of discs attached to a round frame, which helps prevent the belt from sagging. Spiral return rollers are characterized by their spiral-shaped design, providing a larger contact surface area for better belt support. Both types are commonly used in industries like mining and manufacturing, where efficient material handling is vital. These rollers play a crucial role in reducing belt tension, minimizing damage, and ensuring smooth and reliable operation of the conveyor system.</p></p>
