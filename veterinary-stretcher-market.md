<p><p>The veterinary stretcher market can be classified into two main types: foldable and unfoldable. Foldable stretchers are designed for easy storage and transportation as they can be folded into a compact size. They are lightweight and convenient for veterinarians who frequently move around or have limited space. On the other hand, unfoldable stretchers are more sturdy and durable. They are typically used in veterinary clinics or hospitals where space is not a constraint. These stretchers provide stability and support for larger animals, ensuring safe and comfortable transportation.</p></p>
