<p><p>Biometric Point-of-Sale (POS) terminals are used to authenticate individuals' identities before allowing transactions. Fixed POS terminals are installed at specific locations, typically in retail stores. They are integrated into the store's infrastructure and provide permanent authentication solutions. On the other hand, portable POS terminals are mobile and can be carried around by sales personnel. These terminals are wireless and allow for on-the-go authentication, making them suitable for businesses that require flexible transactions outside traditional store settings, such as pop-up shops or food trucks.</p></p>
