<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acrylic Sheet Market can be divided into two main types: Cast Acrylic Sheet and Extruded Acrylic Sheet. Cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and allowing it to harden, resulting in a high-quality, clear product. Extruded acrylic sheets are manufactured by pushing acrylic through rollers to create a continuous sheet, offering a more cost-effective option with slightly lower quality. Both types are popular in various industries such as construction, signage, and automotive due to their versatility and durability.</p></p>
