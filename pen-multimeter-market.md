<p><ul><li>Digital Multimeter</li><li>Pointer Multimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pen multimeters are portable devices used to measure voltage, current, and resistance in electrical circuits. There are two main market types for pen multimeters: digital and pointer multimeters. Digital multimeters have a digital display that shows the measurements in numeric form, providing precise readings and easy-to-read results. On the other hand, pointer multimeters use a moving pointer to indicate the measurements on a scale. While digital multimeters offer advanced features and better accuracy, pointer multimeters are more affordable and convenient for quick measurements in basic electrical tasks.</p></p>
