<p><strong>The Moored Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point Mooring (SPM)</li><li>Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The moored buoy market consists of two main types: Single Point Mooring (SPM) and Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM). SPM involves a single buoy that is connected to an underwater line and anchored to the seabed. This system is commonly used for offloading oil and gas from tankers. On the other hand, MBM consists of multiple buoys arranged in a cluster and connected to each other and a floating production system. MBM is typically used for offshore oil and gas production, allowing for simultaneous loading and unloading operations.</p></p>
