<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optic In-Line Isolators Market is segmented into single stage isolator and two stage isolator. Single stage isolator only has one isolator element, which allows unidirectional transmission of light and offers minimal isolation. On the other hand, two stage isolator has two isolator elements arranged in series. This configuration ensures bidirectional transmission of light and provides higher isolation levels compared to single stage isolators. Both types of isolators play a crucial role in eliminating unwanted reflections and improving optical system performance.</p></p>
