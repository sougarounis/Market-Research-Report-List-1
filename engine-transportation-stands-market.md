<p><strong>The Engine Transportation Stands Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bootstrap Stand</li><li>Non-bootstrap Stand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine transportation stands are used to securely hold and transport aircraft engines. Two main types of stands exist in the market: bootstrap and non-bootstrap stands. A bootstrap stand is equipped with an engine starter, which allows the engine to be started and operated directly on the stand. This simplifies engine testing and maintenance procedures. In contrast, a non-bootstrap stand requires the engine to be installed onto the aircraft before it can be started or operated. Both types of stands offer safe and efficient engine transportation solutions, catering to diverse aircraft maintenance and operational requirements.</p></p>
