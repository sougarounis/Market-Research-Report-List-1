<p><p>The oil-resistant conveyor belt market consists of three main types of belts: fabric conveyor belts, steel cord conveyor belts, and solid woven conveyor belts. Fabric conveyor belts are made of fabric materials and are suitable for medium to light-duty applications. Steel cord conveyor belts are reinforced with steel cords for heavy-duty applications, providing high tensile strength and resistance to impact and abrasion. Solid woven conveyor belts are made of a single ply of fabric resulting in a belt with good tear and impact resistance, ideal for heavy-duty and abrasive materials.</p></p>
