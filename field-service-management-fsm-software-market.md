<p><p>Field Service Management (FSM) software consists of On-premises FSM software and Cloud-based FSM software. On-premises FSM software is installed directly on the company's local servers, allowing for complete control and customization but requiring IT infrastructure and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-based FSM software is hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic software updates, eliminating the need for IT maintenance. Both options cater to different business needs and preferences in managing field service operations efficiently.</p></p>
