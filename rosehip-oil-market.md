<p><p>Rosehip oil is available in two main market types: essential oil and compound oil. Essential oil refers to the pure and concentrated form of oil obtained from the rosehip seeds through steam distillation. It retains the natural fragrance and therapeutic properties of the plant. Compound oil, on the other hand, is a mixture of essential oil with carrier oils or other ingredients, often used to dilute the concentration and enhance the benefits. Both types cater to different preferences and purposes in the skincare and aromatherapy industries.</p></p>
