<p><p>Portal cranes are used in various industries for heavy lifting and transportation of materials. There are different types of portal cranes available in the market, namely gantry cranes and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes are designed with two vertical legs and a horizontal beam, allowing them to move on rails or tracks. On the other hand, half-gantry cranes have only one vertical leg and can be fixed to a stationary structure. Both types of cranes are efficient and versatile, offering solutions for lifting operations in different environments.</p></p>
