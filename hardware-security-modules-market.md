LAN-based HSMs are connected to a network for centralized key management, while PCle-based HSMs are installed directly onto a computer's motherboard for faster processing speed. USB-based HSMs are portable devices that can be easily moved between different systems. Each type offers its own unique benefits and is tailored to specific security needs.</p></p>
