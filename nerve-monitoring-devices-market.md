<p><strong>The Nerve Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EEG</li><li>Evoked Potential</li><li>EMG</li><li>ECoG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nerve monitoring devices market consists of various types such as EEG (electroencephalography), Evoked Potential monitoring, EMG (electromyography), and ECoG (electrocorticography). EEG devices measure electrical activity in the brain, while Evoked Potential devices monitor nerve function in response to stimuli. EMG devices record muscle activity, and ECoG devices monitor brain activity directly from the surface of the brain. Each type of monitoring device serves a unique purpose in assessing and diagnosing neurological conditions, contributing to the growth of the overall nerve monitoring devices market.</p></p>
