<p><p>The Boom Angle Indicator is a device used in construction and various industries to determine the inclination of a boom or jib. There are two common types of indicators available in the market- Ball Type and Bubble Type. The Ball Type features a spherical mass that moves according to the inclination, providing a visual representation of the angle. Bubble Type indicators, on the other hand, utilize a fluid-filled tube with a bubble that moves to indicate the boom's angle. Both types serve the same purpose, providing operators with a clear visual reference for controlling their equipment.</p></p>
