<p><strong>The Boiler Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corrosion Inhibitors</li><li>Scale Inhibitors</li><li>Oxygen Scavengers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boiler water treatment market is classified into three types: corrosion inhibitors, scale inhibitors, and oxygen scavengers. Corrosion inhibitors are chemicals used to prevent corrosion and rusting of boiler equipment, ensuring its longevity and functionality. Scale inhibitors are substances that prevent the buildup of mineral deposits inside boilers, thus improving their efficiency and reducing maintenance costs. Oxygen scavengers are chemicals that eliminate dissolved oxygen from boiler water, as oxygen can cause severe corrosion. These three types of treatment play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and durability of boiler systems.</p></p>
