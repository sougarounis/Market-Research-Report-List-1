<p><strong>The Static Mixers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Mixers</li><li>Pipe Mixers</li><li>Gas Dispersion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static mixers are devices designed to efficiently mix two or more fluids together without the need for moving parts. Channel mixers are static mixers installed inside a channel or a duct to facilitate mixing of fluids flowing through it. Pipe mixers are similar to channel mixers but are inserted directly into a pipe. Gas dispersion systems, on the other hand, are static mixers that are specifically designed to disperse gases evenly within a liquid. These market segments cater to different applications and industries, offering various options for mixing fluids efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
