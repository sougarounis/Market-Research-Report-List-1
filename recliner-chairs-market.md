<p><p>Recliner chairs come in four main types. Wall hugging recliners are designed to save space as they can be placed very close to the wall. Push-back recliners operate by pushing the backrest backward, allowing the user to recline. Massage chairs are equipped with motors that provide various massage functions for relaxation and stress relief. Riser recliners have a mechanism that enables the chair to tilt forward and assist users in standing up. These different types cater to varying preferences and needs, offering a wide range of options in the recliner chairs market.</p></p>
