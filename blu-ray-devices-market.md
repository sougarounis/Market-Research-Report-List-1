<p><p>Blu-ray devices can be classified into two main types: readers and recorders. Blu-ray readers are designed solely for reading Blu-ray discs and retrieving the content stored on them. These devices are commonly used in home entertainment systems or computers for playing movies, games, or other Blu-ray content. On the other hand, Blu-ray recorders have the added functionality of not only reading but also writing (recording) data onto Blu-ray discs. This enables users to create their own Blu-ray discs by recording their own content, such as movies, videos, or data backups.</p></p>
