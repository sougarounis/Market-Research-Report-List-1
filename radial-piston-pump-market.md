<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The radial piston pump market consists of three types: cylinder type, crankshaft linkage, and polygonal transmission. </p><p>In the cylinder type, multiple pistons are arranged radially around a central cylinder, creating a pumping action as they move in and out. </p><p>The crankshaft linkage type uses a crankshaft mechanism to convert rotary motion into reciprocating motion, which drives the pistons to pump fluids. </p><p>In the polygonal transmission type, the pistons are connected to a polygonal shaft that translates their motion into fluid pumping. These different types offer various benefits and are utilized in different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
