<p><ul><li>Dry Seasoning</li><li>Wet Seasoning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BBQ seasoning is available in two main market types: dry seasoning and wet seasoning. Dry seasoning refers to a powdered or granulated blend of spices, herbs, and other flavorings that are commonly rubbed onto the meat before grilling or smoking. Wet seasoning, on the other hand, consists of a liquid or paste-like mixture that is used as a marinade or basting sauce during the cooking process. Both types offer unique flavor profiles and are popular choices among BBQ enthusiasts, allowing them to enhance the taste of their grilled or smoked dishes.</p></p>
