<p><ul><li>Back Closed-back Type</li><li>Back Open-back Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wired workout headphone market is divided into two main types: back closed-back and back open-back. Back closed-back headphones have a sealed design that prevents sound leakage and blocks ambient noise. This enhances sound isolation and bass response, making them suitable for intense workouts and environments with loud external noise. On the other hand, back open-back headphones have perforated ear cups, allowing sound to escape and enabling better audio transparency. They offer a more spacious soundstage and are favored by those looking for a more immersive workout experience with minimal sound isolation.</p></p>
