Wall-mounted nanofiltration water machines are compact and can be easily installed on a wall. Bibcock nanofiltration water machines are attached to a faucet or tap and are suitable for small-scale applications. Pipeline nanofiltration water machines are integrated into the existing water supply system and provide a continuous supply of filtered water. Each type offers specific advantages and is designed to cater to different needs in the market.</p></p>
