<p><ul><li>On-premise SIS</li><li>Cloud-based SIS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-premise SIS refers to a type of Student Information System that is installed and hosted locally on the institution's servers. It requires maintenance and updates to be managed by the institution's IT staff. Cloud-based SIS, on the other hand, is a web-based system that is accessed through the internet and hosted on external servers. It offers greater flexibility and accessibility as it can be accessed from any device with an internet connection. Updates and maintenance are generally the responsibility of the SIS provider, alleviating the burden on the institution's IT staff.</p></p>
