<p><p>The distance meters market consists of three main types: electro-optical distance meters, laser distance meters, and sound wave distance meters. Electro-optical distance meters use light waves to measure distance, making them suitable for indoor applications. Laser distance meters employ laser beams and offer accurate measurements for both indoor and outdoor use. Sound wave distance meters use audio waves to determine distance, making them ideal for measuring underwater or in materials like concrete. Each type of distance meter has unique features and applications, catering to different user needs.</p></p>
