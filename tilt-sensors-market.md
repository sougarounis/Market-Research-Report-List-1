<p><p>Tilt sensors are devices used to measure the tilt or inclination of an object or surface. There are three types of tilt sensors: solid pendulum, liquid pendulum, and gas pendulum. </p><p>Solid pendulum tilt sensors use a solid mass suspended by a hinge or pivot point to measure tilt. Liquid pendulum tilt sensors use a liquid-filled chamber and a float or bubble to indicate tilt. Gas pendulum tilt sensors rely on the movement of gas within a chamber to measure tilt. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications based on its design and principles of operation.</p></p>
