<p><p>Automated pallet conveyor market consists of various types such as drag chain, roller, and others. Drag chain conveyors are designed with interlocking links that move pallets along the conveyor line. They are ideal for heavy loads and can handle both unitized and non-unitized pallets. Roller conveyors use multiple rotating rollers to transport pallets smoothly and efficiently. They provide a flexible and cost-effective solution for moving pallets in warehouses and factories. Other types of automated pallet conveyors may include belt conveyors, gravity conveyors, and vertical reciprocating conveyors, among others.</p></p>
