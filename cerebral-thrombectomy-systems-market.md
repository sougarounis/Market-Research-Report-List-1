<p><p>The Cerebral Thrombectomy Systems market consists of various types of systems used to remove blood clots from the brain. Rotational Thrombectomy Systems employ rotating wires or catheters to remove clots. Aspiration Thrombectomy Systems use suction to extract clots. Other types include clot-dissolving drugs and stent retrievers. These systems play a crucial role in treating conditions like ischemic strokes by efficiently removing clots to restore blood flow.</p></p>
