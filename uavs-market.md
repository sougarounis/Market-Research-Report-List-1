<p><p>The UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) market can be classified into three main types: Rotary Airfoil, Fixed-Wing, and Others. Rotary Airfoil UAVs, also known as rotary-wing or rotorcraft UAVs, are designed with rotating blades that provide lift and maneuverability, making them suitable for vertical takeoff and landing. Fixed-Wing UAVs are designed like traditional airplanes with a fixed-wing structure, enabling them to fly longer distances and at higher speeds. The "Others" category encompasses unconventional UAV designs, such as hybrid or convertible models, that combine elements from both rotary airfoil and fixed-wing configurations.</p></p>
