<p><p>The high-speed camera market consists of two types: high-speed video camera and ultra-high-speed video camera. High-speed video cameras are capable of capturing fast-moving objects in slow motion, typically at speeds ranging from 500 to 10,000 frames per second (fps). They are widely used in sports, scientific research, and industrial applications. On the other hand, ultra-high-speed video cameras have even faster frame rates, often exceeding 10,000 fps. These cameras are primarily utilized in specialized fields like aerospace, engineering, and defense industries for detailed analysis of high-speed phenomena.</p></p>
