<p><p>Solenoid valves are used to control the flow of liquids or gases in various industrial applications. The market for solenoid valves can be categorized into four types: 2-way, 3-way, 4-way, and 5-way valves. </p><p>A 2-way valve has two positions, either open or closed, and is commonly used to control on/off operations. </p><p>A 3-way valve has three ports, allowing for diversion or mixing of fluid flows. </p><p>A 4-way valve has four ports and can be used for both diverting and mixing applications. </p><p>A 5-way valve, with five ports, is versatile and can be used for complex fluid control functions, including diverting multiple flows.</p></p>
