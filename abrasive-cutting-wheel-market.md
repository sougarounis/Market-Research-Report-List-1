<p><p>An abrasive cutting wheel, also known as a cutoff wheel or a disc, is a tool used for cutting and grinding various materials such as metal, stone, and concrete. The cutting action is achieved by the abrasive particles rubbing against the workpiece, which generates heat and friction, resulting in material removal.</p><p>The abrasive cutting wheel market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand from industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing, where cutting and grinding operations are essential.
