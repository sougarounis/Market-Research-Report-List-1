<p><p>Precision electric motors, specifically brushless motors, are classified into two main market types: AC brushless motors and DC brushless motors. AC brushless motors are powered by alternating current and offer efficient and reliable performance. These motors are commonly used in applications such as HVAC systems, industrial machinery, and pumps. On the other hand, DC brushless motors run on direct current and are known for their high torque, speed control, and compact size. They find applications in sectors like robotics, consumer electronics, and automotive industries.</p></p>
