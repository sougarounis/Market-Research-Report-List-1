<p><p>Satellite manufacturing and launch market types include Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Beyond GEO markets. LEO satellites operate at relatively low altitudes, providing global coverage for applications such as Earth observation and communication. GEO satellites are placed at an altitude of 36,000 kilometers, offering continuous coverage over a specific region. MEO satellites operate at medium altitudes and serve applications like navigation systems. Beyond GEO market covers satellites positioned beyond traditional orbits, such as deep space missions or satellites for scientific research. Each market type presents unique opportunities and challenges for satellite manufacturers and launch providers.</p></p>
