<p><p>Chickpeas market consists of two main types: Kabuli and Desi. Kabuli Chickpeas have a larger size, light cream-colored seed coat, and a smooth texture. They are mainly grown in the Mediterranean region, North America, and Australia. Kabuli Chickpeas are commonly used in salads, hummus, and other Mediterranean dishes. On the other hand, Desi Chickpeas are smaller in size, have a darker seed coat, and a rougher texture. They are predominantly grown in the Indian subcontinent and are used in various traditional Indian dishes like curries, snacks, and soups.</p></p>
