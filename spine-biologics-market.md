<p><strong>The Spine Biologics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bone Graft</li><li>Bone Graft Substitute</li><li>Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)</li><li>BMAC (Bone Marrow Aspirate Concentrate)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spine biologics market consists of various types, including bone graft, bone graft substitute, platelet-rich plasma (PRP), and bone marrow aspirate concentrate (BMAC). </p><p>Bone graft is a procedure that involves transplanting bone tissue from one area to another in order to stimulate bone growth. Bone graft substitutes, on the other hand, are materials that mimic the properties of real bone and can be used as an alternative to traditional bone grafting. </p><p>Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) is a concentration of platelets obtained from the patient's own blood, which contains growth factors that promote tissue healing and regeneration. </p><p>BMAC, or bone marrow aspirate concentrate, involves extracting bone marrow from the patient's hip and concentrating it to obtain a high number of adult stem cells that have the potential to aid in bone regeneration and repair.</p></p>
