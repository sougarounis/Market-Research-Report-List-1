<p><p>The body armor vest market is segmented into two main types: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor is typically made from flexible materials like fabric and provides protection against lower impact threats such as handguns and knives. On the other hand, hard armor is composed of rigid materials like ceramic or steel plates, offering higher protection against more powerful threats like rifles and shrapnel. Both types of body armor vests are designed to safeguard individuals, such as military personnel and law enforcement officers, from potential ballistic and stab injuries.</p></p>
