<p><p>Swimming pool algaecides are used to prevent and treat algae growth in pools. Quat pool algaecides contain quaternary ammonium compounds that effectively kill and control algae. Polymer/polyquat pool algaecides use polymers and quat compounds to control and prevent algae growth. Metallic pool algaecides contain copper or silver ions that inhibit algae growth. Sodium bromide is a non-metallic algaecide that works by oxidizing and killing algae. These different types of algaecides offer pool owners various options to effectively maintain algae-free swimming pools.</p></p>
