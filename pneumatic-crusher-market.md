<p><p>The pneumatic crusher market can be categorized into two types: handheld and fixed. Handheld pneumatic crushers are portable and can be easily operated by hand. They are commonly used for small-scale crushing tasks and can be conveniently carried to the desired location. On the other hand, fixed pneumatic crushers are stationary machines that are permanently installed in a specific location. Due to their larger size and more powerful crushing capabilities, fixed pneumatic crushers are suitable for heavy-duty crushing operations in industries like mining and construction.</p></p>
