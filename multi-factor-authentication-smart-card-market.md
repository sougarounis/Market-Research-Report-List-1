<p><p>Multi-Factor Authentication Smart Card market comprises two types: Contact and Contactless. Contact smart cards require physical contact with a card reader to function. They provide high security and are commonly used in access control systems. Contactless smart cards, on the other hand, utilize radio frequency technology for communication, enabling users to simply wave their card near a reader for authentication. These cards are convenient and widely used in public transport systems, payment applications, and identification purposes, offering a seamless user experience while ensuring security.</p></p>
