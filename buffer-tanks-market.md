<p><p>Buffer tanks are commonly used in heating and cooling systems to store excess energy and maintain a consistent temperature. Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT) are specifically designed for cooling applications and help balance the load on chillers, ensuring adequate chilled water supply during high-demand periods. On the other hand, Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT) are used in heating systems to store extra hot water and provide additional supply when the demand exceeds the boiler's capacity. Both types of buffer tanks contribute to system efficiency, reliability, and optimal performance.</p></p>
