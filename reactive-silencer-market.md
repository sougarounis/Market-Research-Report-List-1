<p><p>The reactive silencer market can be categorized into two types: resonant silencer and expansion silencer. Resonant silencer is designed to reduce noise emissions by using a resonator chamber to cancel out specific frequencies. On the other hand, expansion silencers function by allowing the exhaust gas to expand gradually, which helps in reducing noise levels. Both types are widely used in various industries to minimize noise pollution and maintain a quieter environment.</p></p>
