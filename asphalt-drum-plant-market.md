<p><p>An asphalt drum plant is a type of machinery used in the construction industry to produce asphalt mixtures. It consists of several components such as a cold aggregate feeder, a drum dryer, a burner, a dust collector, and a hot aggregate elevator. The process of producing asphalt mixtures in a drum plant involves drying and heating the aggregates, adding asphalt binder, and then mixing it all together to produce the final product.</p><p>The future outlook for the asphalt drum plant market seems promising due to increasing infrastructure development activities across the globe.
