<p><p>Horticultural LED lighting refers to the use of light-emitting diodes (LEDs) for growing plants and crops indoors. Firstly, the rising demand for locally grown and pesticide-free crops has led to an increased adoption of indoor farming techniques. LED lighting enables vertical farming and greenhouse cultivation, providing sustainable and efficient alternatives to traditional farming methods.</p><p>Additionally, the declining cost of LED lighting systems and the increasing awareness about energy-efficient and eco-friendly lighting solutions have contributed to the market's growth. LED lights consume less energy compared to traditional lighting options and have a longer lifespan, reducing operational costs for farmers and horticulturists.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in LED technology, including the development of tunable and full-spectrum LED lights, have enhanced their effectiveness in promoting plant growth and boosting crop yields. These innovations have led to increased adoption across various horticultural applications such as fruits, vegetables, herbs, and flowers.</p><p>The horticultural LED lighting market is also witnessing several trends.
