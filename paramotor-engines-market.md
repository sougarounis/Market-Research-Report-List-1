<p><strong>The Paramotor Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Stroke</li><li>4-Stroke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paramotor Engines are used to power paramotors, which are lightweight aircraft with a motor and a propeller. There are two types of paramotor engines: 2-stroke and 4-stroke. 2-stroke engines are simpler, lighter, and more compact, making them popular for paramotors. They require a mixture of fuel and oil for lubrication. On the other hand, 4-stroke engines have separate compartments for fuel and oil, making them more efficient and requiring less maintenance. They are heavier and often used by paramotor pilots who prioritize fuel economy and longer flight durations.</p></p>
