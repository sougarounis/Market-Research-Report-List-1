<p><strong>The Allergy Diagnostic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blood Testing</li><li>Skin Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Allergy diagnostic testing includes two main types: blood testing and skin testing. In blood testing, a blood sample is taken from the patient and tested for specific antibodies that indicate allergies. This method provides a comprehensive analysis of a wide range of allergens. On the other hand, skin testing involves applying small amounts of allergens on the patient's skin, usually through pricks or patches, and observing any allergic reaction. This type of testing is useful for identifying immediate allergic reactions and determining the severity of allergies.</p></p>
