<p><p>Process Analytical Technology (PAT) refers to the use of advanced analytical techniques in various industries for real-time monitoring and control. Spectroscopy, chromatography, particle size analysis, capillary electrophoresis, and other techniques make up the PAT market. Spectroscopy involves the study of interaction between matter and electromagnetic radiation, while chromatography separates and analyzes components in a mixture. Particle size analysis measures the size distribution of particles, and capillary electrophoresis separates and detects components based on their charge. Other techniques such as thermal analysis and mass spectrometry are also used in the PAT market.</p></p>
