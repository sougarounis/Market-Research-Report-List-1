<p><p>Underfloor heating is a popular heating solution that comes in two main types: hydronic heating and electric heating. Hydronic heating involves a system of pipes installed beneath the floor, through which hot water or other fluids are circulated to provide warmth. On the other hand, electric heating involves the use of electrical cables or mats installed beneath the floor to generate heat. Both systems offer the advantage of providing even heat distribution across the room, while hydronic heating is often considered more energy-efficient and electric heating is easier to install and control.</p></p>
