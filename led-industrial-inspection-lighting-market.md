<p><p>LED Industrial Inspection Lighting Market includes various types of lighting sources used in industrial inspection applications. </p><p>- Ring Light Source: A circular light that surrounds the camera lens and provides uniform illumination from various angles.</p><p>- Bar Light Source: A linear light source that emits a narrow beam of light and is often used for long and narrow inspection areas.</p><p>- Back Lights: Positioned behind the object being inspected, these lights create a silhouette effect and help to identify defects or imperfections.</p><p>- Coaxial Light Source: Directs light towards the object's surface. It is used to reduce glare and shadows, ensuring clear images.</p><p>- Others: This category includes different types of lighting sources used in specific applications or industries, such as dome lights or spotlights.</p></p>
