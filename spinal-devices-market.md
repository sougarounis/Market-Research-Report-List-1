<p><strong>The Spinal Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spinal Fusion</li><li>Non-fusion Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal devices market can be categorized into two main types: spinal fusion and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion involves the use of devices such as plates, screws, and cages to stabilize and join two or more vertebrae together. This helps in reducing pain and improving spinal stability. Non-fusion products, on the other hand, include artificial discs and dynamic stabilization devices that aim to preserve spinal motion while treating conditions such as degenerative disc disease. Both types of devices play a crucial role in the treatment of various spinal disorders and conditions.</p></p>
