<p><p>The Medium Power Transformer market is divided into two types: Dry Type Transformer and Oil Immersed Transformer. Dry Type Transformers do not require a liquid for cooling and insulation, making them safer and more environmentally friendly. They are typically used in buildings and areas where fire safety is a concern. On the other hand, Oil Immersed Transformers use oil as a cooling and insulation medium, making them efficient for high-power applications. They are commonly used in industrial and utility settings where high voltage levels are needed.</p></p>
