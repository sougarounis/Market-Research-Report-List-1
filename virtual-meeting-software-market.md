<p><p>Virtual meeting software market types can be categorized as cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software refers to applications that are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and easy accessibility from any location, making it suitable for businesses of all sizes. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed locally on the company's servers and managed by their IT department. It provides more control over data and security but requires maintenance and upfront costs. Both types offer various features for conducting virtual meetings and collaboration.</p></p>
