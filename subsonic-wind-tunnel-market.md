<p><p>Subsonic wind tunnel market offers two main types: open wind tunnels and closed wind tunnels. Open wind tunnels are characterized by having an open test section, which allows for easy access to the model being tested. They are suitable for studying aerodynamics of complete vehicles and large-scale models. On the other hand, closed wind tunnels have closed test sections, providing a controlled environment for testing smaller models with higher accuracy. Closed wind tunnels are typically used for more precise investigations into aerodynamic properties, such as airflow patterns and drag coefficients.</p></p>
