<p><ul><li>Total IgE</li><li>Specific IgE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inhalant Allergy Test Panel market encompasses two types of tests: Total IgE and Specific IgE. Total IgE test measures the overall level of immunoglobulin E antibodies in the blood, which helps identify an individual's general allergy sensitivity. On the other hand, Specific IgE test examines the presence of antibodies specific to particular allergens, allowing for the diagnosis of specific allergies. These tests aid healthcare professionals in determining the types of allergens causing a patient's allergic reactions and developing appropriate treatment plans.</p></p>
