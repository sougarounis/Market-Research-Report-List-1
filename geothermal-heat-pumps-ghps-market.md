<p><p>Geothermal Heat Pumps (GHPs) are categorized into three main market types based on their loop configurations: Vertical Closed Loop, Horizontal Closed Loop, and Open Loop systems. Vertical Closed Loop systems use pipes drilled vertically into the ground, Horizontal Closed Loop systems have pipes buried horizontally, and Open Loop systems utilize water from a nearby source. Each system type offers unique advantages and applications, allowing for customization based on the availability of land, water resources, and project requirements.</p></p>
