<p><p>Germanium lenses are available in various types in the market such as Plano-Concave, Plano-Convex, Concave-Convex, and Aspheric lenses. Plano-Concave lenses have one flat surface and one inward-curved surface, while Plano-Convex lenses have one flat surface and one outward-curved surface. Concave-Convex lenses have one inward-curved and one outward-curved surface. Aspheric lenses have a non-spherical surface. These different types of Germanium lenses cater to a wide range of optical applications with specific requirements.</p></p>
