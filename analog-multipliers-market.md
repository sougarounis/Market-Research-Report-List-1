<p><p>Analog multipliers are devices used to multiply two input signals together to generate an output signal. In the market, there are three types of analog multipliers: single quadrant, two quadrant, and four quadrant. </p><p>Single quadrant analog multipliers can only multiply signals that have the same sign, either positive or negative. Two quadrant analog multipliers can multiply signals with both positive and negative signs, but they cannot generate a negative output. Four quadrant analog multipliers can multiply signals with any combination of positive and negative signs, allowing them to generate both positive and negative outputs.</p></p>
