Die Sinking EDM involves creating cavities or molds in metal by using a shaped electrode and sinking it into the material. Hole Drilling EDM creates precise holes in metal using a rotating electrode. These different types of EDM machines cater to the specific needs of industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics, providing high accuracy and fine finishes in the machining process.</p></p>
