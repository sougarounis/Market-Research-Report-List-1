<p><p>The eco-friendly plastic bags market includes three major types: Polylactic acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA is made from fermented plant sugars and has similar properties to traditional plastic, but it is compostable and biodegradable. PHA is produced by bacteria and can be used in various applications, although it is still relatively expensive. Starch blends are created by combining starch with a biodegradable polymer, offering a more affordable option for eco-friendly bags. These alternatives aim to reduce plastic waste and have a lower environmental impact.</p></p>
