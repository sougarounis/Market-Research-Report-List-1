<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Top Coat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Train coatings refer to protective layers applied to trains to enhance their durability and aesthetics. The market for train coatings can be divided into two main types: primer and top coat. Primers are the first layer applied to the train surface, providing corrosion resistance and ensuring better adhesion for subsequent coats. Top coats are the final layer applied to enhance color, gloss, and overall appearance, as well as providing additional protection against environmental factors. Both primer and top coat play essential roles in preserving the integrity and longevity of train coatings.</p></p>
