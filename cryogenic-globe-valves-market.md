<p><p>Cryogenic globe valves are categorized based on the type of connection they have - butt welding type, flange type, and thread type. Butt welding type valves are designed to be welded directly into pipes, providing a sturdy connection. Flange type valves are connected using bolts and flanges, offering flexibility and easy installation. Thread type valves are screwed directly into pipes, making them suitable for small-scale applications. These types of valves are commonly used in cryogenic applications, where materials need to withstand extremely low temperatures.</p></p>
