<p><p>The Silicon Carbide (SiC) Diodes market includes two types of diodes: Single Diodes and Dual Diodes. Single diodes are electronic components with a single PN junction, allowing current to flow in one direction. They are commonly used in applications where low power consumption and high-speed switching are required. On the other hand, dual diodes consist of two interconnected diodes within a single package, enabling current to flow in two opposite directions. Dual diodes are commonly used in power supplies and rectifier circuits where bidirectional current flow is necessary.</p></p>
