<p><p>The wearable jetpack market consists of two main types: hydrogen peroxide driven and fuel driven jetpacks. Hydrogen peroxide driven jetpacks use hydrogen peroxide as the main propellant, which decomposes into steam and oxygen to create thrust. These jetpacks are generally lightweight and provide short-duration flights. On the other hand, fuel-driven jetpacks use various types of fuels, such as gasoline or kerosene, to generate thrust and provide longer flights. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in terms of flight duration and performance.</p></p>
