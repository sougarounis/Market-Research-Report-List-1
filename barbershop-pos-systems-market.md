<p><strong>The Barbershop POS Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barbershop POS systems can be categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are web-hosted platforms that store data on remote servers, providing flexibility and accessibility to barbershops from any location with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed directly on the barbershop's local servers, allowing for full control and customization of the software but requiring maintenance and upgrades to be managed locally. Both types offer advantages and it depends on the specific needs and preferences of the barbershop.</p></p>
