<p><p>Robotic Pharmacy Prescription Dispensing Systems are classified into two main types: Centralized Dispensing Systems and Decentralized Dispensing Systems. Centralized Dispensing Systems involve a central location where medications are stored and dispensed to multiple pharmacies or healthcare facilities. This system optimizes inventory management and reduces costs. On the other hand, Decentralized Dispensing Systems allow for medication dispensing within each individual pharmacy or healthcare facility. This system provides quicker access to medications and better customization options for each facility's needs. Both systems aim to improve efficiency and accuracy in medication dispensing.</p></p>
