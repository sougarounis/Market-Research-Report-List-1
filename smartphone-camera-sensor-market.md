<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Smartphone Camera Sensor Market includes two major types: Front-illuminated Sensor and Backside-illuminated Sensor. Front-illuminated Sensors are commonly used in conventional camera sensors, where the electronics and wiring are located in front of the light-sensitive area, leading to decreased efficiency in capturing light. Conversely, Backside-illuminated Sensors have their electronics and wiring positioned behind the light-sensitive area, resulting in improved light gathering capability. This allows for enhanced low-light performance, higher image quality, and improved dynamic range, making it a preferred choice for smartphone cameras.</p></p>
