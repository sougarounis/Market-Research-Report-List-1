<p><p>The Baggage Conveyor System market includes various types of conveyors for efficiently transporting baggage at airports. The Delivery Conveyor is responsible for moving bags from the check-in counter to the sorting area. The Injection Conveyor transfers bags from the sorting area to the correct baggage bay for loading onto planes. The Queue Conveyor organizes baggage in a systematic line for security checks. Lastly, the Extended Queue Conveyor is an extension of the queue conveyor, allowing for additional baggage to be placed in line.</p></p>
