<p><p>A Consent Management System (CMS) is a software solution that enables organizations to effectively manage user consents and preferences regarding the collection, use, and sharing of their personal data. Organizations are recognizing the importance of obtaining valid consents from their customers and implementing CMS solutions to streamline their consent management processes.</p><p>Factors driving the market growth include the rising adoption of digital marketing and advertising techniques, which require proper consent management, the growing awareness of data privacy rights among individuals, and the need for organizations to build trust and transparency with their customers. Additionally, the increasing adoption of cloud computing and the emergence of advanced technologies like AI and machine learning are further bolstering the demand for CMS solutions.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several trends. One such trend is the integration of consent management with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions to enhance data governance and security.
