<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum suction and fan adsorption are two types of robotic window cleaners available in the market. Vacuum suction cleaners utilize a powerful suction mechanism to attach themselves to the window surface, allowing them to navigate and clean efficiently. On the other hand, fan adsorption cleaners use a fan-like device to create a vacuum between the cleaner and the window, enabling them to adhere and move smoothly while cleaning. Both types offer different technologies for cleaning windows, providing consumers with options based on their specific cleaning needs.</p></p>
