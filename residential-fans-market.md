<p><p>Residential fans are categorized into two main types: AC Residential Fans and DC Residential Fans. AC residential fans operate using alternating current and are directly powered by the electricity grid. These fans are typically more powerful and suitable for larger spaces. On the other hand, DC residential fans are powered by direct current, usually from batteries or solar panels. DC fans are more energy-efficient, cost-effective, and suitable for smaller spaces. Both types serve the purpose of providing ventilation and cooling in residential settings, but differ in terms of power source and efficiency.</p></p>
