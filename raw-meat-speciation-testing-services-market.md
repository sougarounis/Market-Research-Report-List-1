<p><strong>The Raw Meat Speciation Testing Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>ELISA</li><li>Other Molecular Diagnostic Tests (LC-MS/MS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The raw meat speciation testing services market is segmented into three types: PCR, ELISA, and other molecular diagnostic tests (LC-MS/MS). PCR (polymerase chain reaction) is a technique used to amplify and detect specific genes, allowing for the identification of meat species in a sample. ELISA (enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay) involves the use of antibodies to detect and quantify meat species. Other molecular diagnostic tests, such as LC-MS/MS (liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry), use advanced technology to identify and quantify meat species based on their molecular profiles. These testing methods help ensure the authenticity and quality of meat products in the market.</p></p>
