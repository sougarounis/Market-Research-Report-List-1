<p><p>The hand knitting machine market is categorized into two types: circular machines and flat knitting machines. Circular machines are designed to produce knitted fabrics in a seamless tubular form, commonly used for making items like socks and hats. On the other hand, flat knitting machines create knitted fabrics by moving the yarn back and forth in a straight line, enabling the production of flat panels or shaped pieces used for making garments such as sweaters and scarves. These two types of machines offer different knitting techniques to cater to various knitting needs.</p></p>
