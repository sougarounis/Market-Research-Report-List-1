<p><p>The laser engraver market can be classified into various types, including CO2 laser engravers, fiber laser engravers, diode laser engravers, and Nd:YAG laser engravers. CO2 laser engravers are versatile and commonly used for engraving on non-metal materials. Fiber laser engravers are known for their high precision and speed, making them suitable for metal engraving. Diode laser engravers are compact and cost-effective, commonly used for small-scale engraving on various materials. Nd:YAG laser engravers offer high-power engraving capabilities, making them ideal for industrial applications involving metal marking and engraving.</p></p>
