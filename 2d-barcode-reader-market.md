<p><p>A 2D barcode reader, also known as a 2D imager, is a device used to scan and decode two-dimensional barcodes. This allows for the storage of much more information, including text, URLs, and even multimedia.</p><p>The market for 2D barcode readers has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing adoption of digitalization and automation in various industries, such as retail, healthcare, transportation, and logistics, has been driving the demand for 2D barcode readers. These readers provide a faster and more accurate method of data capture compared to manual entry, reducing errors and increasing efficiency.</p><p>Additionally, the rise of e-commerce and online shopping has further propelled the growth of the 2D barcode reader market. With the increasing number of online transactions, there is a need for quick and secure identification and tracking of products, which can be efficiently achieved through 2D barcode scanning.</p><p>The latest trends in the 2D barcode reader market include the integration of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML).
