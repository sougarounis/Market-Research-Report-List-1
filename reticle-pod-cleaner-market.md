<p><p>The reticle POD cleaner market consists of two main types: EUV POD cleaner and non-EUV POD cleaner. EUV POD cleaners are specifically designed to clean reticles or photomasks used in extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography, a cutting-edge technology in semiconductor manufacturing. These cleaners utilize specialized techniques and materials to ensure effective removal of contaminants without causing damage to the delicate EUV reticles. On the other hand, non-EUV POD cleaners are used for traditional reticles used in other lithography processes, offering efficient cleaning solutions tailored to the needs of non-EUV lithography.</p></p>
