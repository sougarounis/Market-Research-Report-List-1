<p><p>Single Point (or Mixer) Open Loop Type Systems in the Automotive LPG System market involve a simple vaporizer connected to the engine's intake manifold. Single Point (or Mixer) Closed Loop Type Systems feature an electronic control unit that continuously adjusts the air-fuel ratio based on feedback from oxygen sensors. Other types of systems in the market include sequential fuel injection systems, where LPG is injected directly into each cylinder. These systems offer more precise control over fuel delivery and can result in improved fuel efficiency and lower emissions.</p></p>
