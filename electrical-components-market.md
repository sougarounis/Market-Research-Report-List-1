<p><ul><li>Active Components</li><li>Passive Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical components market can be classified into two main types: active components and passive components. Active components include devices that can amplify, control, or generate electrical signals, such as transistors, diodes, and integrated circuits. They require an external power source to function. On the other hand, passive components do not require power and include devices like resistors, capacitors, and inductors. These components help with electrical energy storage, filtering, and signal passivity. Both active and passive components play crucial roles in various electrical systems and applications.</p></p>
