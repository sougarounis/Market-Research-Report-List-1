<p><p>Continuous screen changers are devices used in various manufacturing processes to remove impurities and contaminants from the material being processed. These screen changers come in different types, namely single piston and double piston. </p><p>Single piston screen changers use a single piston to automatically switch the screens as needed, ensuring uninterrupted production. This type is generally suitable for applications with lower pressure requirements. </p><p>On the other hand, double piston screen changers utilize two pistons, enabling faster and more efficient screen changing. This type is often employed in high-pressure applications where quick and frequent screen changes are necessary.</p></p>
