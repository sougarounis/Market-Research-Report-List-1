<p><p>The electric scooter lift and carrier market can be classified into two types: interior and exterior. Interior lifts are designed to be installed inside a vehicle, allowing users to transport their scooters conveniently and securely. These lifts are typically installed in vans, SUVs, or minivans and can be operated manually or electronically. On the other hand, exterior carriers are mounted on the exterior of the vehicle, such as the rear bumper or roof. These carriers are ideal for vehicles with limited interior space and are often manually operated. Both types offer different solutions for scooter transportation, depending on the user's needs and preferences.</p></p>
