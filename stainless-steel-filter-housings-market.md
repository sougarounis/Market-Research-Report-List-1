<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Filter Housings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side-entry Type</li><li>Top-entry Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel filter housings are available in two main types: side-entry and top-entry. Side-entry filter housings are designed with an inlet and outlet connection on the same side of the housing, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. On the other hand, top-entry filter housings have an inlet connection at the top and an outlet connection at the bottom. This design allows for easy access and maintenance, as the filter can be easily removed from the top. Both types cater to different space requirements and offer convenience for different applications.</p></p>
