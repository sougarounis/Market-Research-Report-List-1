<p><p>Ceramic composite membranes are used in various industries for separation and filtration processes. The market for these membranes can be categorized into three types: flat-sheet membrane, pipe membrane, and other markets. </p><p>Flat-sheet membranes consist of thin sheets with a dense structure and are commonly used in applications that require high rejection rates for filtration. Pipe membranes, on the other hand, are tubular in shape and are mainly used for gas separation or liquid filtration processes. The other market category includes different types of ceramic composite membranes used in niche applications such as wastewater treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing. These membranes offer high structural integrity and excellent separation efficiency.</p></p>
