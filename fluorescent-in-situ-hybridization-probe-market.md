<p><p>The Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) Probe market includes various types such as mRNA and miRNA probes. mRNA probes are designed to bind specifically to messenger RNA molecules in cells, allowing researchers to study gene expression levels. miRNA probes, on the other hand, are used to detect and analyze microRNA molecules within cells, which play a crucial role in post-transcriptional gene regulation. Both mRNA and miRNA probes are vital tools in molecular biology research, enabling scientists to investigate gene function, disease mechanisms, and potential therapeutic targets.</p></p>
