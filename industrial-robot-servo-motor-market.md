<p><p>The industrial robot servo motor market consists of two main types: AC servo motors and DC servo motors. AC servo motors utilize alternating current as their power source, offering high accuracy and speed control, making them ideal for applications requiring precise positioning. On the other hand, DC servo motors rely on direct current and provide superior torque controllability. Both AC and DC servo motors are widely used in industrial robots to enable efficient automation, enhance productivity, and improve manufacturing processes.</p></p>
