<p><p>Automotive Polyamide Resin (PA) market types include Reactive Type and Nonreactive Type. Reactive Type polyamide resins are designed to chemically react with other compounds in order to create a durable and high-performance material suitable for automotive applications. Nonreactive Type polyamide resins, on the other hand, do not undergo chemical reactions and are instead used as coatings or adhesives in automotive components. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the strength, durability, and functionality of automotive parts.</p></p>
