<p><p>Fiber optic MEMS switches are categorized into two main types: single-mode switches and multimode switches. Single-mode switches are specifically designed for use with single-mode fibers, which are capable of transmitting signals over long distances. Multimode switches, on the other hand, are compatible with multimode fibers that are suitable for shorter distance transmissions. Both types play a crucial role in the fiber optic industry by facilitating the efficient and reliable switching of optical signals to enable seamless communication in various applications.</p></p>
