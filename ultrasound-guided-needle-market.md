<p><strong>The Ultrasound Guided Needle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EUS-FNA</li><li>EUS-FNB</li><li>Others (EBUS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasound Guided Needle Market includes various types such as EUS-FNA (Endoscopic Ultrasound-Fine Needle Aspiration), EUS-FNB (Endoscopic Ultrasound-Fine Needle Biopsy), and others like EBUS (Endobronchial Ultrasound). EUS-FNA involves using a thin needle to extract tissue or fluid samples from organs adjacent to the gastrointestinal tract. EUS-FNB is similar, but uses a larger needle for obtaining samples. EBUS, on the other hand, incorporates ultrasound technology to guide the needle into the airways for sampling. These techniques aid medical professionals in diagnosing and staging various diseases, particularly cancers.</p></p>
