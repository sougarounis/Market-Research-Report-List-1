<p><p>The Nanoparticle Analysis market consists of various types of analysis methods like Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS), Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis (NTA), Laser Diffraction Method (LDM), and Others. DLS measures the size distribution of particles in a liquid suspension. NTA visualizes and tracks nanoparticles in real-time using light scattering. LDM determines particle size by analyzing the diffraction pattern produced by laser light. Other methods include electron microscopy and field-flow fractionation. All these techniques aid in the accurate characterization and understanding of nanoparticles in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and environmental sciences.</p></p>
