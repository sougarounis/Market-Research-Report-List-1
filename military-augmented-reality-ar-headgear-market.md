<p><p>The military augmented reality (AR) headgear market includes various types of head-mounted displays (HMD), monitor-based, and video see-through HMD. Head-mounted displays are worn on the head and provide augmented reality information directly to the user's eyes. Monitor-based systems use a combination of screens and cameras to display AR content. Video see-through HMDs use transparent screens to overlay virtual information onto the user's view of the real world. These different types of headgear cater to the specific needs and requirements of military applications and provide soldiers with enhanced situational awareness and information during operations.</p></p>
