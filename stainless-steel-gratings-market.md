<p><p>Stainless steel gratings market can be segmented into various types including welded grating, swage locked grating, press locked grating, and others. Welded grating is manufactured by welding the bearing bars and cross rods together. Swage locked grating features a rigid construction with high strength. Press locked grating is made by pushing cross rods through bearing bars and permanently locking them in place. Other types of gratings include riveted and close mesh gratings which cater to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
