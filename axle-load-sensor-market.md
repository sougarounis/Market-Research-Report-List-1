<p><p>Axle load sensors are used to measure the weight distribution and load on axles of vehicles. There are different types of axle load sensors available in the market. Spring-mounted sensors utilize springs to sense the load on the axles. Pressure mounted sensors use pressure sensors to measure the load. Location mounted sensors are installed at specific locations on the vehicle to detect axle load. Air-mounted sensors use air pressure to measure the load on the axles. These sensors provide valuable data for ensuring vehicle safety, efficiency, and compliance with weight restrictions.</p></p>
