<p><p>The Scintillator Screen market is divided into two main types: Gadolinium Oxy-Sulphide (GOS) and Cesium Iodide (CsI). GOS screens are known for their high efficiency in converting X-ray radiation into visible light, making them ideal for high-resolution imaging applications. On the other hand, CsI screens offer a higher level of sensitivity and better image quality, particularly in low-dose imaging situations. Both types of screens play a crucial role in medical imaging, security screening, and industrial inspection applications.</p></p>
