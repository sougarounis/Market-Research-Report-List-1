<p><p>Typhoid fever vaccines market consists of different types. Live attenuated vaccines are made from weakened but live bacteria, providing long-term protection. Conjugate vaccines combine the bacterial antigen with a carrier protein, enhancing immune response, especially in children. Capsular polysaccharide vaccines contain the outer coating of the bacteria, stimulating the immune system. Other types include Vi-rEPA, which targets the Vi antigen, and Vi-rEPA/PS, a combination of the Vi antigen and outer coating. Each vaccine type offers varying degrees of protection against typhoid fever.</p></p>
