<p><p>Texturized vegetable protein (TVP) is available in three main types - soy, wheat, and pea. Soy TVP is made from soybeans and is the most common type, providing a high protein content and versatile usage in various dishes. Wheat TVP is derived from wheat gluten and has a chewy texture, often used as a meat substitute in vegetarian recipes. Pea TVP is made from yellow peas and is sought after for its mild flavor and high nutritional value. All three types provide a sustainable and plant-based protein alternative to meat.</p></p>
