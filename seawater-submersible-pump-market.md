<p><p>The Seawater Submersible Pump market includes two main types: Single Stage Submersible Pump and Multi Stage Submersible Pump. Single Stage Submersible Pump refers to a pump that has only one impeller to generate pressure and is typically used for shallow water applications. On the other hand, Multi Stage Submersible Pump includes multiple impellers stacked together to create higher pressure, making it suitable for deep water or high-pressure environments. Both types play significant roles in efficiently pumping seawater, catering to different requirements and depths.</p></p>
