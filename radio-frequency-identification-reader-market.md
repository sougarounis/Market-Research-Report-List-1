<p><p>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Reader market can be categorized into two types: Fixed RFID Readers and Handheld RFID Readers. Fixed RFID Readers are stationary devices used to capture and process data from RFID tags within a specific range. They are commonly used in applications like inventory management and access control systems. On the other hand, Handheld RFID Readers are portable devices that allow users to read and write data on RFID tags while on the move. They are widely used in logistics and retail industries for inventory management and asset tracking purposes.</p></p>
