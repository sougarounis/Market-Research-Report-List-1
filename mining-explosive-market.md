<p><p>Mining explosive market types include ammonium nitrate explosives (powder), ANFO, and emulsion explosives. Ammonium nitrate explosives are commonly used in mining operations due to their high stability and power. ANFO, a mixture of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, is a popular choice for its cost-effectiveness. Emulsion explosives consist of a mixture of oxidizers, fuels, and sensitizers, providing a versatile option for various mining applications. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of stability, cost, and performance in mining operations.</p></p>
